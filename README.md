# Project Title

Sales Performance & Customer Profitability Dashboard

### Project Overview

This project delivers an end-to-end Power BI solution designed to analyse business performance across sales, profit, customer behaviour, and growth trends.
The dashboard provides business for decision-makers through interactive visualisations and DAX calculations.

### Business Objectives

- Identify revenue drivers
  
- Analyse profit vs discount impact

- Evaluate customer retention behaviour

- Track sales growth over time



### Data Cleaning & Transformation

#### Cleaned and Transformed using Power Query:

- Removed duplicates

- Standardised column data types

- Created calculated columns

- Merged columns

- Built star schema data model

- Created calendar table for time intelligence



### Tools Used

- Power BI

- Power Query

- DAX



 ### Key Insights

- Consumer customers contribute the largest share of total sales, indicating strong B2C revenue dependence.

- Technology products generate the highest revenue, making them the primary sales driver.

- The Western region records the strongest sales performance compared to other regions.

- Sales revenue is highly concentrated among a small number of top-performing products.

- Sales trends show seasonal fluctuations with noticeable peaks during later months of the year.

- Average order value is relatively high, suggesting customers tend to purchase higher-value items per transaction.



 ### Business Recommendations

- Strengthen marketing and loyalty programs targeting consumer customers to increase repeat purchases and customer lifetime value.

- Increase investment and product availability within the technology category to maximize revenue growth from the highest-performing segment.

- Implement targeted promotional and distribution strategies to improve sales performance in underperforming regions such as Central and South.

- Reduce reliance on a small number of top-performing products by promoting mid-range products and introducing cross-selling opportunities.

- Align marketing campaigns and inventory planning with seasonal sales trends to maximize revenue during peak demand periods.



### Conclusion

  - This project demonstrates the use of Power BI to transform raw sales data into meaningful business intelligence by combining data cleaning, data modelling, and advanced DAX calculations to uncover trends,
  in customer behaviour, product performance, and regional sales, ultimately supporting strategic and data-driven decision-making.
